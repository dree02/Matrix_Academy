Courses We Offer

At Matrix Academy, we take pride in offering a wide range of courses that cater to students from 8th to 12th grade who are affiliated with the CBSE board.
We believe that every student has the potential to achieve academic success, and our experienced faculty members work tirelessly to ensure that each student receives the attention and support they need to excel. Our courses are designed to be interactive, engaging, and comprehensive, helping students to not only master key concepts but also develop critical thinking skills and a love for learning.
Whether you are looking for a comprehensive course to prepare for the board exams or specialized coaching for competitive exams, we have something for everyone. At Matrix Academy, we are committed to helping students reach their full potential and achieve their academic goals.

8th grade

Matrix Academy offers CBSE-aligned courses for 8th graders in Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, and Hindi. Our experienced faculty employs innovative techniques, ensuring personalized attention, assessments, and feedback for a strong grasp of core concepts, setting students up for success in higher grades and life-changing opportunities.

9th, 10th grade

Matrix Academy offers comprehensive CBSE board exam courses for 9th and 10th-grade students in major subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Science, and English. With our experienced faculty providing personalized attention and regular assessments, students develop a robust foundation for future studies. Through diverse teaching methods and varied aids, we foster holistic learning and the development of crucial life skills.

11th, 12th grade

Matrix Academy offers comprehensive CBSE board exam courses for 11th and 12th-grade students in major subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Accountancy, Business Studies, and Economics. Our experienced faculty employs engaging discussions and regular assessments, nurturing a deep understanding of subjects and enhancing study skills for academic excellence.

Our Centers

These centers have been designed to provide an ideal learning environment for students, with well-equipped classrooms, experienced teachers, and comprehensive study material. Both centers are easily accessible, and offer a safe and secure environment for students to study and learn. Our team of dedicated teachers and staff are committed to providing quality education to students from 8th to 12th grade.
